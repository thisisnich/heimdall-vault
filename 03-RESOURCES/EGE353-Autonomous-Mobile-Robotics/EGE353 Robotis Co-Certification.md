# Robotis Co-Certification Announcement

> **Official Notice**

Dear Students,

We are pleased to announce that students taking EGE353 will be awarded the **Certificate of Competency in Autonomous Robots Integration & Programming**, jointly issued by NYP and Robotis.

## Requirements
- Attain a passing grade for the EGE353 module
- Completion of all module assessments

## Certificate Details
- **Issuing Authority:** Nanyang Polytechnic & Robotis
- **Certification:** Certificate of Competency in Autonomous Robots Integration & Programming
- **Module:** EGE353 Autonomous Mobile Robotics

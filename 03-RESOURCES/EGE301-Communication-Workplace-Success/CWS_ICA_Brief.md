## ICA 1 — Research on Workplace Cultures

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Group |
| **Weighting** | 30% |
| **Due** | Week 4 |

This ICA assesses your ability to identify cultures at workplaces. What kind of (engineering) company would you really want to work for when you graduate? Do you know the company culture, and do you think you can fit in and succeed there?

### Task

**Research** a company of your choice and provide a write-up (**total 3 pages max**).

---

### Part A — Company Culture *(max 2 pages)*

Your write-up should be based on facts or logical and reasonable interpretation of evidence such as:

- The CEO's personality and vision for the company
- Mission statement
- Key values, beliefs or perceptions
- Policies, rules or regulations
- Corporate structure – hierarchy and reporting structure
- Common staff / management practices
- Physical and IT environments, facilities and amenities
- Reaction to accidents and critical situations
- Staff reviews or insights
- Events and activities, such as outings, celebrations and ceremonies

You **must** include company and research materials such as photos, graphs and infographics as supporting evidence in the **Appendix***.

---

### Part B — Key Interpersonal Skills *(max 1 page)*

Based on the workplace culture you have identified:

a. Suggest **3** interpersonal skills essential for succeeding at that workplace.  
b. Explain your suggestions.

---

### Guidance

Which are some good companies to research? Here are some suggested websites:

1. [Great Place to Work](https://greatplacetowork.com.sg/)
2. [HRM Asia — Singapore's Best Places to Work](https://hrmasia.com/singapores-best-places-to-work-revealed/)
3. [The Straits Times — Best Employers Singapore 2022](https://www.straitstimes.com/best-employers-singapore-2022)

> **Note:** Remember to give proper credit to your reference sources. You are allowed to add an extra Appendix/Reference/Citation page to your report if necessary*.

## ICA 2 — Role-play on Workplace Relationships

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Group |
| **Weighting** | 20% |
| **Due** | Week 7 |

This ICA assesses your ability to demonstrate effective interpersonal skills to manage work relationships.

### Task

**Role-play two** of the scenarios below. Present on the conflict management and interpersonal skills demonstrated in the scenarios.

---

### Scenarios

**Scenario 1** — You are working in a friend's company. Your friend is the CEO. One month after joining, you are recruited to a highly regarded project team because of your specialisation in school. Some teammates pass inappropriate remarks about your involvement due to your connection to the CEO.

**Scenario 2** — Two interns have joined your department. One of the main tasks you have given them is to manage the company's social media account. They are very creative and have generated a lot of content and viewership. However, they always refuse to work on other tasks, citing that they are busy with maintaining the success of the social media account.

**Scenario 3** — Your department is preparing an important report. All of you, except for one colleague, have been assigned various tasks. Your department suspects the manager is favouring that colleague who is also his gym buddy. A few of you decide to confront the manager about this.

**Scenario 4** — Your office has a small pantry that can only accommodate one person at any time. This has created tension among staff, and results in an argument on who gets to sit in the pantry during lunch break. The senior staff say it is their right to use the pantry as they have been longer at the company.

---

### Part A — Role-Plays *(your 2 recorded clips should total 5 mins max)*

Form a team of 4–6. Your team will pick any **TWO** scenarios from the list above, and have every member participate in role-playing the scenarios.

In each scenario, the role-playing should clearly illustrate:
- The tension (such as caused by an expressed struggle or unethical behaviour)
- How it is resolved through appropriate and effective interpersonal skills

The resolution of the situation must be logical and reasonable, and such that it leads to improved workplace relationships.

---

### Part B — Debrief and Reflection *(max 5 mins in total)*

After the two role-plays, have **TWO** team spokespeople present on:

1. The reasons for your choice of the style(s) used in managing each of the scenarios. For example, why was the *Avoidance style* used? Was it because of the need to save time, or was it because of any particular workplace belief the characters hold, such as "The boss is always right"? You should refer to the unit on **Conflict Management** to prepare this part.

2. The key interpersonal skills used in managing the situation at each of the role-plays. For example, you can reference the verbal/non-verbal communication, listening skills and show of empathy, as shown in the role-plays. It is good to be specific and point out extracts of the conversations during the role-plays.

---

### Peer Evaluation

There is a Peer Evaluation component for this ICA where you will be graded based on your teammates' evaluations of your efforts and contributions.

## ICA 3 — Negotiating, Service and Networking Skills

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Individual |
| **Weighting** | 50% |
| **Due** | Week 13 |

This ICA assesses your ability to demonstrate and apply negotiation, service, and networking skills to **your work/daily life**.

### Task

**Write a short reflection on 2 topics** and **present your reflection**.

---

### Part A — Written Reflection on 2 Topics *(max 400 words in total)*

Choose **2 out of the 3** topics below and respond to the guiding questions to explain and write your reflection. Review your tutorials/e-learning and think about what you will immediately apply to your work or daily life.

---

**Topic 1: Negotiation Skills** *(max 200 words)*

- Who are you negotiating with?
- What are you negotiating about?
- What are his/her needs and fears you can address, to assure him/her better?
- How can you identify those needs and fears, rather than positions?
- How can you negotiate better with this person?
- How can you be a more ethical negotiator?

---

**Topic 2: Networking Skills** *(max 200 words)*

- What skills and knowledge do you possess which can add value to others and the market?
- What deeper skills and knowledge can you acquire? How can you acquire them?
- Who can you help, to learn and to connect with others?
- What domains can you become an expert in?
- How can you make your expertise and interests known to others?
- How can you upskill and network better with others?

---

**Topic 3: Customer Service Skills** *(max 200 words)*

- What is the service situation you encountered?
- How can you understand the perspective of this person who may be behaving negatively/aggressively towards you?
- How can you be more understanding, generous, and compassionate towards him/her?
- What issues/suffering may he/she be experiencing?
- How can you be kind to him/her (so you may learn the art and science of self-compassion)?
- What kind gestures/language can you use?

---

> **Remember:** Choose **ONLY 2** out of three topics and write **NOT MORE than 400 words in total**. Make sure you demonstrate understanding and application to your work/life. **Do not include the questions in your submission.**
>
> **Note:** Plagiarism checker Turnitin will be activated. Please make sure your similarity score is **below 25%**. You can submit unlimited times.

---

### Part B — Present Your Reflection *(max 3 mins)*

Present your reflections to the class.

# Micron Business Unit Reorganization

> **Source:** [Benzinga - Chip Maker Micron Splits Into Four Business Units To Accelerate AI Strategy](https://www.benzinga.com/25/04/44867319/chip-maker-micron-splits-into-four-business-units-to-accelerate-ai-strategy)

## Executive Summary

Micron Technology Inc. (NASDAQ: MU) restructured into four specialized business units to better address rising AI-driven demand for memory and storage solutions. The strategic reorganization enhances customer focus across key market segments.

---

## Reorganization Details

### Effective Date
- **Implementation:** Q4 Fiscal Year 2025
- **Announcement:** April 2025
- **Strategic Purpose:** Accelerate AI strategy and market responsiveness

### New Business Unit Structure

#### 1. Cloud Memory Business Unit (CMBU)
- **Market Focus:** Cloud computing and data center memory solutions
- **AI Application:** Large-scale AI training and inference workloads
- **Customer Segment:** Cloud service providers, hyperscale data centers

#### 2. Core Data Center Business Unit (CDBU)  
- **Market Focus:** Enterprise data center memory solutions
- **AI Application:** Traditional enterprise AI and analytics workloads
- **Customer Segment:** Enterprise customers, private data centers

#### 3. Mobile & Client Business Unit (MCBU)
- **Market Focus:** Mobile device and client computing memory
- **AI Application:** Edge AI, mobile AI processing, client-side workloads
- **Customer Segment:** Smartphone manufacturers, PC makers, device OEMs

#### 4. Automotive & Embedded Business Unit (AEBU)
- **Market Focus:** Automotive and embedded systems memory
- **AI Application:** Autonomous driving, IoT, industrial AI
- **Customer Segment:** Automotive manufacturers, industrial equipment makers

## ICA 1 — Research on Workplace Cultures (Group, 30%)

**Due: 15 May 2026, 23:59** (Week 4)

**Task:** Research a company of your choice — **max 3 pages total**
- **Part A (max 2 pages):** Company culture — CEO personality, mission, values, structure, practices, critical incidents
- **Part B (max 1 page):** 3 interpersonal skills essential for succeeding at that company — explain why
- Must be an **engineering, SG-based company**
- No two groups can pick the same company — first come first serve
- Supporting evidence (photos, graphs, infographics) go in **Appendix** (doesn't count to page limit)

**How to get an A:** Identify the key message/culture you want to communicate → find evidence to support it

*Example: SIA CEO flew to injured passengers after SQ321 turbulence incident — shows valuing safety and passengers*

## Global Inclusion Commitments

At Micron, bold and global inclusion commitments help create opportunities for all to "go beyond the norm and achieve greatness."

### Commitment 1: Expand Talent Pool
- **Focus:** Increase hiring from nontraditional pathways
- **Goal:** Expand the talent pool beyond traditional sources
- **Strategy:** Diverse recruitment channels and approaches

### Commitment 2: Fair Pay and Benefits
- **Focus:** Drive fair pay and benefits for all
- **Goal:** Ensure equitable compensation across workforce
- **Strategy:** Regular compensation analysis and adjustments

### Commitment 3: Community & Inclusion
- **Focus:** Champion advocacy and strengthen culture of inclusion
- **Goal:** Create supportive community environment
- **Strategy:** Employee resource groups and advocacy programs

### Commitment 4: Investments
- **Focus:** Engage with financial institutions for cash management
- **Goal:** Unlock opportunities for all through strategic investments
- **Strategy:** Partnerships with diverse financial institutions

### Commitment 5: Supplier Ecosystem
- **Focus:** Build competitive and resilient supply chain
- **Goal:** Expand supplier network diversity
- **Strategy:** Inclusive supplier development programs

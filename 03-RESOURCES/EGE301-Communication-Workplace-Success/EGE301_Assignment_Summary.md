- **Technology Focus:** Leading Micron during exciting period of AI and computing architecture changes
- **Innovation Driver:** Passionate about leveraging AI technologies for new memory and storage opportunities
- **Industry Advocate:** Active promoter of STEM education and youth discovery programs
- **Communication Style:** Active on social media (LinkedIn, Twitter @micronceo) for stakeholder engagement

#### Strategic Approach
- **Market Adaptation:** Responsive to computing architecture changes and AI opportunities
- **People Development:** Champions opportunities for future technology leaders
- **Global Perspective:** Board member of CDW Corporation and Semiconductor Industry Association

- **Experience:** Three years with company by 2026
- **International Posting:** 18-month stint in Hiroshima, Japan
- **Rarity:** Unusual posting opportunity for young engineer

#### Supportive Management Experience
> **Key Insight:** "They were always ready to understand what I was going through. They didn't just throw solutions at me – they took a step back, listened and understood what I was feeling first. That meant a lot."

## Team Building & Workplace Culture

### Monthly Bonding Activities
#### Structured Time Investment
- **Frequency:** Once per month
- **Format:** Afternoon set aside for employee bonding
- **Purpose:** Step away from desks, spend time together

#### Notable Events
- **Laser Tag:** Johor Bahru outing with cross-department colleagues
- **Amazing Race:** Gardens by the Bay adventure activity
- **Birthday Celebrations:** Regular team recognition events

#### Cultural Impact
> **Connection Benefits:** "The connections and friendships formed over such events make it easier to ask for help, speak up and look out for one another at work."

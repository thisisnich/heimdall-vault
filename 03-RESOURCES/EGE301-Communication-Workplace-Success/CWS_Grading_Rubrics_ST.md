## ICA 1: Research on Workplace Cultures (Group) — 30% (30 marks)

| Criteria | Excellent | Very Good | Good | Satisfactory | Needs Improvement |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Content and Organisation** *(15 marks)* | Shows comprehensive knowledge of subject matter. Organised in a logical sequence, with points that are well-elaborated. Interesting, relevant, and meaningful. | Shows good knowledge of subject matter. Organised in a logical sequence, with points that are sufficiently elaborated. Adequately interesting, relevant, and meaningful. | Shows some knowledge of subject matter. Organised in a logical sequence, with some elaboration. Interesting, relevant, and meaningful at some parts. | Ideas were run-of-the-mill. Little critical thought. Organised for some readability, with points briefly or scantily explained. Somewhat interesting, relevant, and meaningful at parts. | Shows a lack of thought. Organised poorly and hampers comprehension, with no elaboration of points. Uninteresting and irrelevant content. |

Evaluates and improves on the ideas of others. | **Occasionally:** Puts in effort and produces quality work. Punctual for meetings. Shares, listens to, and contributes ideas. ## ICA 3: Individual Reflection Paper (Individual) — 50% (50 marks)

*Paper [400 words]: 35 marks + Presentation [3 mins]: 15 marks*

### Paper Criteria (35 marks)

| Criteria | Excellent | Very Good | Good | Satisfactory | Needs Improvement |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Content** *(20 marks)* | To a very high degree, interesting, relevant, and meaningful reflections and applications relating to module and negotiation, service and networking skills. | To a high degree, interesting, relevant, and meaningful reflections and applications relating to module and negotiation, service and networking skills. | To some degree, interesting, relevant, and meaningful reflections and applications relating to module and negotiation, service and networking skills. | Adequately interesting, relevant, and meaningful reflections and applications relating to module and negotiation, service and networking skills. | Uninteresting and irrelevant content which lack meaningful reflections and applications relating to module and negotiation, service and networking skills. |
| **Language and Coherence** *(15 marks)* | Very clear and comprehensible reflections and applications. | Mostly clear and comprehensible reflections and applications. | Sufficiently clear and comprehensible reflections and applications. | Adequately clear and comprehensible reflections and applications. | Incoherent and incomprehensible reflections and applications. |

### Presentation Criteria (15 marks)

| Criteria | Excellent | Very Good | Good | Satisfactory | Needs Improvement |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Content and Organisation / Delivery and Command of Language** *(15 marks)* | Presentation is organised in a logical sequence. All main points are well elaborated. Speaker appears very confident. Displays effective use of eye contact, posture, gestures, and vocal expressiveness. Great fluency and excellent use of English. | Presentation is organised in a logical sequence. All main points are sufficiently elaborated. Speaker appears quite confident. Displays relatively good use of eye contact, posture, gestures, and vocal expressiveness. Good fluency with minor errors in English. | Presentation is organised in a somewhat logical sequence. Some main points are sufficiently elaborated. Speaker appears confident. Displays adequate use of eye contact, posture, gestures, and vocal expressiveness. Fluent with some glitches in delivery. | Presentation is organised in a sequence that could be improved. Some main points are elaborated. Speaker appears rather uncomfortable. Displays some use of eye contact, posture, gestures, and vocal expressiveness. Fluency can be better, but the audience's understanding is not affected. | Presentation is organised in a haphazard manner. Most main points lack elaboration. Speaker appears very uncomfortable. Displays poor use of eye contact, posture, gestures, and vocal expressiveness. Poor fluency or pronunciation hampering understanding. |

# **HSX302 HEALTHY AND RESILIENT FAMILIES**

**ICA 1 Assignment (40%)**

# **LEARNING OUTCOMES**

* Identify and evaluate their own family communication patterns
* Apply theoretical concepts to personal experience by comparing and analysing their family communication patterns with the 4 theoretical family types
* Reflect on the interplay between their personal values and family communication patterns, and articulate how their values shape their family interactions or how their family communication influences their values

# **INSTRUCTIONS**

* This assignment consists of **THREE (3)** questions. **Answer all questions**.
* Minimum **250** **words in total**.
* Refer to marking rubric.
* You may refer to lecture materials.

# **SUBMISSION**

* Type your answer in **Microsoft Word document format** **(.doc / .docx)**.
* Save your document as “***AdminNo.\_YourName***”.
* Submit assignment via **Turnitin on Brightspace**.

# **PLEASE TAKE NOTE**

* This assignment is due on **WEEK 7 (7 June 2026 by 11:59pm)**.
* Deadline for submission of ICA must be strictly adhered to.
* Late submission may be subject to penalty or awarded zero (“0”) mark.
* Your written work must be an individual effort.
* Students are reminded that use of artificial intelligence or any automated writing assistance tools is prohibited in the completion of this assignment. Copying another person’s work without proper referencing is plagiarism. Sharing of your work with others is also **not** permitted. Such acts may be subjected to disciplinary action.
Any assignment not received **by the stated deadline** will be deemed as a late submission. Based on NYP regulations: Marks will be deducted - 5% of the total base marks allocated for the assignment will be deducted for each calendar day the assignment is late. If the assigned work is not received **within three calendar days** following the due date, the assignment will be reviewed for feedback but will not be assigned a mark or grade.
**Question**
**Family Communication and Values?**
- 1. Complete a short self-survey of your family communication pattern here:
<https://www.psychologies.co.uk/test/how-does-your-family-communicate/>
- 1. Based on your results, discuss whether it tallies with your own evaluation of your family type as covered in **Lecture 4 Communication (I)** – is it a protective family, pluralistic family, laissez-faire family, or consensual family? Give at least **TWO 2** examples to elaborate.
- 2. Next, identify one personal value (e.g integrity) which you hold close to, and explain why it is so important to you.

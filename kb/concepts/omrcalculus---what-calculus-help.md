## 📱 Study Apps & Tools

### **Flashcard Apps**

#### 1. **Anki** ⭐ HIGHLY RECOMMENDED
- **Link:** https://apps.ankiweb.net/
- **Platform:** Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, Web
- **Cost:** FREE (iOS app is $25 but worth it)
- **Why:** Spaced repetition algorithm, highly effective
- **Use for:** Memorizing formulas, Java methods, theorems

#### 2. **Quizlet**
- **Link:** https://quizlet.com/
- **Platform:** Web, iOS, Android
- **Cost:** Free (premium optional)
- **Why:** Easy to use, pre-made decks available
- **Use for:** Quick flashcard creation

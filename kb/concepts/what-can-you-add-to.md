# What Can You Add To

- What can you add to the environment?
<!-- Slide number: 11 -->
# Think about this
Think of a time you were criticised and felt really angry about it. What can you do something good about it?
Is it about acknowledging any feedback as good feedback?
Is it about helping you to explain or to clarify, and to learn?
Is it about being grateful for the opportunity to engage?
How would you (take a deep breath) communicate, constructively?

# Think about this
Recall an experience with someone who triggered your negative reaction by her tone, posture, odd request, or misunderstanding.
How may you practice understanding, generosity, and compassion?
What may this person be facing you may not be aware of?
How may you be kinder, and what can you say to soothe her (and your) nerves?
What may be happening in their life?

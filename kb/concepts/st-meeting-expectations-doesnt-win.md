# St Meeting Expectations Doesnt Win

- st meeting expectations. Doesn’t win loyalty.
What people hope for. Delivered just the way another person likes it. Extra fast, flexible, or friendly. Brings people back.
Something special. Unexpected. Stands out from crowd. Customers come back again and again. Understands customer.
Astonishing and fantastic. Unforgettable. Legendary. Fondly shared by customers. By colleagues with pride. Deeply passionate. Extraordinary service.

# When things go wrong, avoid common thinking:
“It’s not my fault.”
“Our policy isn’t clear.”
“I don’t have time to fix this.”
“I can’t do anything right now.”

# Service champions do the following…
Take responsibility and action to improve situation
Says “I will fix this for you”
Empower and inspire others
Don’t blame circumstances
Look for steps forward
![Picture 3](Picture3.jpg)
### Notes:
They don’t play victim, and they look for positive experiences, build teamwork, increase pride, and improve communication.

# Shes When Pb1 Is Pressed

- shes. When PB1 is pressed eight times, an amber LED flashes. When PB1 is pressed twelve times, a red LED flashes. After this, a left gantry is raised. Two seconds later, the gantry is lowered and all LEDs are turned off.

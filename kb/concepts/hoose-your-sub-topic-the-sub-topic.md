# Hoose Your Sub Topic The Sub Topic

- hoose your *Sub-topic.
(*The Sub-topic must be related to your Main topic.)
e.g. Main topic: Japanese Dining Etiquette & Sub-topic: Table Manners
e.g. Main topic: Japanese Public Holidays & Sub-topic: New Year’s Day
- 2) Prepare your own slides for your presentation.
► Duration: Within 3 minutes
► Number of slides: Between 7 to 9 slides in total, as follows:
- 1.  Cover Page:
o  Main topic & Sub-topic
o  Module Group
o  Your Name and Admin Number
- 2.  Contents Pages:
o  3 to 5 slides minimum (this is where you present the core content of your topic)
- 3.  References/Resources Page:
o  List of URLs, books, and other resources you referred to for your research
- 4.  Last Page:
o
- 5.  Key Takeaways:
“Thank you” message
o  This slide will help you during the Q&A session after your presentation
Pictures, illustrations, and photos can be used to help demonstrate your points. <!-- id:cc3de2c2-fce4-493a-b6cf-c51d1abfb18d ts:2026-05-17 07:49 -->

# Ways   Coming Of Age

- ways
- Coming of Age Day (Seijin no Hi) celebrates transition to adulthood
- Traditional attire (furisode, hakama) creates beautiful cultural displays
- Ceremonies (Seijinshiki) bring communities together
- Deep historical roots dating back to 8th century
- Balances modern legal changes with traditional customs
- Emphasizes family, community, and personal growth

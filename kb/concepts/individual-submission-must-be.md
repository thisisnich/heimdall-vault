## 🔑 Key Concepts

### Unit 1 — Workplace Culture & Ethics
- Organisational culture: beliefs, perceptions, values
- Who shapes culture: CEO, HR, Staff
- Culture by business type (small family → global/tech)
- How to identify culture: norms, rituals, critical incidents
- Workplace ethics: rules, professionalism, respect
- Unethical behaviours: bullying, harassment, credit claiming

### Unit 3 — Negotiation Skills
- BATNA (Best Alternative to Negotiated Agreement)
- Power of Information (A.S.K. framework)
- Use of Status (credentials, experience, expertise)
- Use of Social Capital (trust, relationships)

### Unit 4 — Networking Skills
- Networking types: Internal vs External
- Purpose: Support, Knowledge, Career Growth (S-K-Y)
- Networking concepts: Relationship building, communication, strategic connections, follow-up
- Networking strategies: Identify contacts, join organizations, attend conferences, use social media
- "Tell Me About Yourself" formula

# Acting Negotiator B Summarizes Negotiator

- acting)
Negotiator B summarizes Negotiator A’s explanation
Negotiator A confirms that the summary is correct, or makes corrections
Above is repeated, until both agree the message heard, and sent, are the same
A and B exchange roles. Practise again.

### Notes:
Breakdowns in negotiation occur, due to dysfunctional listening
People tend to assume/interpret, based on their values and logic system
Critical to choose, and create, a non-distracting environment/keep quiet, to listen
Be encouraging, and repeat the other person’s key words, and phrases
Clarify: “So you feel…”
Ask the speaker to repeat his message, if needed

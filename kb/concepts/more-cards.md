## 🎨 HTML in Answers

You can use HTML in your answers for formatting:

```javascript
answer: "<ul><li>Point 1</li><li>Point 2</li></ul>"
answer: "<strong>Bold text</strong> and <em>italic</em>"
answer: "<table><tr><th>Header</th></tr><tr><td>Data</td></tr></table>"
```

## 🏷️ Optional Fields

- **critical**: `true` for must-know cards (shows red badge)
- **chapter**: Group cards by chapter (e.g., `"2.1"`, `"2.2"`)
- **category**: Subject classification

## 💡 Tips

- Keep questions concise and clear
- Use HTML for better formatting in answers
- Mark critical/important cards with `critical: true`
- Group related cards with chapter numbers
- Test your deck after creating it!

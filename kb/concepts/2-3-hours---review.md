3. **Know Your Calculator** - Learn all functions thoroughly

5. **Memorize Key Series** - Taylor series for e^x, sin(x), cos(x), ln(1+x)
6. **Time Management** - Practice under timed conditions
7. **Review FRQs** - Study scoring guidelines from past exams

# Understand   Dont Just Copy

- Understand
- Don't just copy — actually read and comprehend
- Compare with your lecture notes
- Identify what's new vs. what you already know

### 2. Verify
- Check against official course materials
- Confirm formulas/code with textbooks
- Ask instructor if unsure about accuracy

### 3. Synthesize
- Combine senior's approach with your understanding
- Create your own examples based on the concept
- Write in your own words

### 4. Link in Your Notes
Add a reference block in your note:

```markdown
> 📖 **Senior Reference:** [[Senior Notes/EGE320 ESD/Pointers-Guide|Pointer Arithmetic Guide]]
> 
> **Key insight:** Senior's memory diagram helped clarify pointer-to-pointer concept.
> **Difference:** They used older C syntax; I'm using C11 standard.
> **Action:** Practice with their examples, then create my own.
```

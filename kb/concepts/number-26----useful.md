# Useful questions for professional networking
Review the five people you spent the most time and energy with, for the last six months. Are you glad about the influence they have on you?
Who are the two person you know, who do not know each other, whom you know would benefit from knowing each other?
If you lost your job suddenly, who are the people you would email to seek advice from?

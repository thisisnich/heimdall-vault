# Ofdma Multiple Narrow Orthogonal

- - **OFDMA:** Multiple narrow orthogonal bands (4G/LTE)
- **SDMA:** Directional beams (energy efficient)

### Quality of Service (QoS)
- **Latency:** Packet travel time + interaction time
- **Reliability:** Guaranteed data delivery
- **Security:** Encryption, authentication, privacy

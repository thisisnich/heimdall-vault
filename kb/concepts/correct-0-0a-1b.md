## 💡 Tips for Effective Studying

### **Flashcards Best Practices**
1. **Study daily** - Even 10-15 minutes helps
2. **Shuffle regularly** - Prevents memorizing order
3. **Say answers out loud** - Reinforces learning
4. **Create your own** - Making cards helps retention
5. **Review difficult cards** - Focus on weak areas

### **MCQ Quiz Best Practices**
1. **Read explanations** - Even for correct answers
2. **Time yourself** - Practice under exam conditions
3. **Review mistakes** - Understand why you got it wrong
4. **Retake quizzes** - Spaced repetition works!

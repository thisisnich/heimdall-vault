# Siness Card First Accepting The

- siness card first
Accepting the business card:
- 1. Don't put the card into the holder or in a
pocket right away

# EXTRA

Simultaneous exchange:
- 1. Use your right hand to offer
your card
- 2. Use your left hand to receive one

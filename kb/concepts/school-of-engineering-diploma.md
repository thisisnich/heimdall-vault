## Wireless Communication & Networking (EGE321)

### Tutorial 1: RF Fundamentals

> 📚 **Related:** [[Lesson1c|Chapter 1]] | [[Lesson2c|Chapter 2]]

---

1. Define wireless communication. What are the advantages and disadvantages of wireless communication?
	1. Flexibility, Easy installation, mobility, 
	2. Security, planning, interference

2. Name and explain the three types of transmission modes in data communication and their applications.
	1. Simplex: only one way -> TV/aircon remotes remotes, radios
	2. Half Duplex: two ways but only one way at a time -> Walkie Talkies, wireless keyboards and mouse
	3. Full Duplex: two ways and both simultaneously -> Mobile phones

3. Draw the basic building block of an RF system and explain briefly the function of each block.
	1. Transmitter (TX): converts information(baseband) into a transmissible form, which is then transmitted through the channel(medium), which the receiver (RX) receives and converts back into usable data.

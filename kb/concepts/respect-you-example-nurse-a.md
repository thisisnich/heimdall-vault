## Identifying the Type of Negotiation Strategies

### Scenario 1
Alex, a soon-to-graduate engineering student, gets two job offers:
- One from a well-known Company A (lower pay)
- One from a smaller Company B (higher pay)

Alex uses the better offer to ask the well-known Company A for more pay and benefits.

**Which strategy is being used?** Give rationale.
- 1) BATNA
- 2) Use of Status
- 3) Use of Social Capital
- 4) Use of the Power of Information

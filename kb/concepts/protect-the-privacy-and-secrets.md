# Protect The Privacy And Secrets

- Protect the privacy and secrets of others
- No gossiping

---

### 💬 Let's Discuss

> Is it gossip to talk about these?
> - Speculate whether Diyanna is expecting a child?
> - Spread the news that Kumaran and Rachel are getting married before their public announcement?
> - Ask colleagues if a client is being sued for sexual harassment?

---

### Unethical Behaviour

**Should I Lie…**
- …to leave work urgently to attend to private matters?
- …to hide a costly error?
- …to save face even when I need help?

✅ Better approach: Admit to mistakes, get official leave, seek help early.

---

**Should I Claim Credit…**
- …if I contributed only some initial ideas?
- …if my involvement was only for a short while?
- …if my role wasn't important or critical?

✅ Better approach: Give credit where it is due. Avoid "I" and "My" — use "We" and "Ours".

---

**Should I Do Non-Office Related Work…**
- …which distracts me from office work?
- …which are mainly for personal use?
- …which wastes office resources?

✅ Better approach: Avoid distractions, keep to priority list. Can affect health and performance.

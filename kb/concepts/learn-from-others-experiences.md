# Learn From Others Experiences

- - Learn from others' experiences and share best practices to grow together

### Y – Your Career Growth
- Open doors to mentorship, job opportunities, and long-term professional development
  (Jacobs, De Vos, Stuer, & Van der Heijden, 2019)

---

## Networking at the Workplace

### Relevance

**1. Project Outcomes**
- Improves and enhances project outcomes by allowing quick access to expert opinions and resources

**2. Collaboration**
- Promotes interdisciplinary collaboration, improving overall project delivery and customer experience

**3. Professional Development**
- Encourages continuous learning and staying updated with the latest development, new methods, processes and technology
  (Jacobs, De Vos, Stuer, & Van der Heijden, 2019)

---

## Networking Concepts: Enhancing Connections

### 1) Relationship Building
- **Build trust and respect**
  - Form genuine connections with working team members, superiors and colleagues from other departments
- **Work closely as a team**
  - Daily collaboration strengthens teamwork and support
- **Improve project outcomes**
  - Strong relationships lead to better communication and improved project outcomes
  (Rajamani & Iyer, 2023)

### 2) Communication Skills
- **Listen actively**
  - Understand others' concerns and perspectives during handovers or team discussions
- **Share clearly**
  - Use concise, accurate language when reporting project updates or changes in situation

# Effective networking should include
Professional allies
Weaker ties and acquaintances
![Picture 3](Picture3.jpg)
### Notes:
Professional allies are in your corner when you are under conflict or stress. They brainstorm career options with you and are people you trust and proactively try to work with. They are your sources of feedback on projects. They review life goals/plans with you, and number 8-10 strong at most.
Weaker ties and acquaintances are friendly but not full-on friends. They contact occasionally and exchange light-weight professional favours. <!-- id:55a490db-a9dc-4b0b-8f7c-e86ba5314b60 ts:2026-05-17 07:49 -->

# Andard To Never Go Around

- andard to never go around people's backs or be deceitful in my actions.

Integrity matters to me because it forms the foundation of all relationships. When I am honest and transparent, others can trust me, and this trust is essential for meaningful connections both personally and professionally. Choosing honesty over convenience or self-interest has helped me build credibility and respect from others. Even in difficult situations where telling the truth might be uncomfortable, I prioritize being truthful because I believe that maintaining integrity is more valuable in the long run than short-term gains or avoiding confrontation.

This value has been reinforced by my family upbringing, where I was consistently taught to act with honesty and to treat others with respect.

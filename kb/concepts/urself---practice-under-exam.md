**After Study Session (20 min)**

**Before Bed (10 min)**

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### **Apps won't open in Obsidian?**
- Open them directly in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
- They work standalone, no internet needed!

### **Want to use on mobile?**
- Transfer HTML files to your phone
- Open in mobile browser (Safari, Chrome)
- Works offline!

### **Lost your progress?**
- Apps don't save progress between sessions
- This is intentional - fresh practice each time
- For tracking, use a separate note in Obsidian

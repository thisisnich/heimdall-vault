# Itive Connections   Value Mentorship

- itive connections
- **Value Mentorship**
  - Recognise mentors who have shaped your learning or inspired your growth
  (Rajamani & Iyer, 2023)

### 2. Join Professional Organisations
- **Expand Your Network**
  - Connect with peers, mentors, and leaders in the field
- **Access Opportunities**
  - Get updates on events, scholarships, and career resources
- **Stay Professionally Engaged**
  - Stay informed, involved, and inspired through e.g. student chapters

**Discuss:** What are some associations, communities, clubs, etc. that support your career aspirations?

### 3. Attend Conferences and Workshops
- Allows you to stay updated on the latest practices and advancements
- Provides opportunities to connect with professionals across various specialties

### 4. Use Social Media Platforms
- Engage actively on platforms like LinkedIn, Twitter and professional forums
- To stay updated and connected
- Use the industry and government initiatives to connect with peers, mentors and potential employers
- Join LinkedIn focused groups to participate in discussions on industry trends and career opportunities
  (Utz & Breuer, 2019)

## Networking: "Tell Me About Yourself"

### A 3-Part One-Sentence Formula

**1. Who You Are**
- Your name and current role in 1 simple sentence

**2. What You Do**
- Add Specificity:
  - What Problems do you Solve?
  - What are you currently working on?
  - What do you specialize in?

# Ative Solutions Time Management

- ative solutions

### Time Management
- Makes time work for him
  - Doesn't let pressure get to him, and has great patience
  - Makes time for his friend when seeking a deal

### Active Listening
- Listens actively
  - Doesn't get distracted by how you feel
  - Builds rapport and demonstrates understanding by reflecting what you are saying and feeling

### Assertiveness
- Asserts your needs
  - Uses assertive language and behavior to state what you think, want, and feel
  - Chooses words carefully and watches body language

### Exploration
- Explores options
  - Increases the number of variables and possibilities for more options for mutual gain

### Verification
- Verifies what has been agreed
  - Goes over every point of agreement by summarising on paper
  - Makes plans to meet/settle future differences

### Relationship Building
- Builds long-term working relationships
  - Is reliable, rational, and receptive, no matter how others behave

### Adaptability
- Adapts his strategy for the situation
  - Thinks win-win all the time!

# Us With Humor   Why

- us with humor
- **Why:** Makes difficult topics approachable
- **Use for:** Motivation, different perspective

---

### **Textbooks & Books**

#### 1. **Barron's AP Calculus** ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- **Cost:** ~$20
- **What:** Comprehensive review book
- **Why:** Good explanations, practice tests
- **Use for:** Review, practice tests

#### 2. **Princeton Review: Cracking the AP Calculus BC Exam**
- **Cost:** ~$20
- **What:** Test prep book
- **Why:** Good strategies, practice tests
- **Use for:** Alternative to Barron's

#### 3. **5 Steps to a 5: AP Calculus BC**
- **Cost:** ~$20
- **What:** Study guide with practice
- **Why:** Multiple study plan options
- **Use for:** Structured review

#### 4. **Stewart's Calculus** (Optional, expensive)
- **Cost:** ~$200+ (or find used/PDF)
- **What:** University calculus textbook
- **Why:** Comprehensive, rigorous
- **Use for:** Deep dives (not necessary)

### **Practice Resources**

#### 1. **College Board Past FRQs** ⭐ ESSENTIAL
- **Link:** https://apcentral.collegeboard.org/courses/ap-calculus-bc/exam/past-exam-questions
- **What:** All past free response questions with scoring guidelines
- **Why:** MUST practice these, understand rubrics
- **Use for:** FRQ practice (do 50+ FRQs before exam)

#### 2. **Albert.io - Calculus BC** (Paid)
- **Link:** https://www.albert.io/ap-calculus-bc
- **Cost:** ~$10-20/month
- **What:** Practice questions with explanations
- **Use for:** Additional practice, weak area identification

- **Barron's AP Calculus** or **Princeton Review AP Calculus BC**

## 🎯 Exam Structure

**Total Time:** 3 hours 15 minutes  
**Section I:** Multiple Choice (45 questions, 105 min, 50% of score)
- Part A: 30 questions, 60 min (no calculator)
- Part B: 15 questions, 45 min (calculator required)

**Section II:** Free Response (6 questions, 90 min, 50% of score)
- Part A: 2 questions, 30 min (calculator required)
- Part B: 4 questions, 60 min (no calculator)

**Content Breakdown:**
- Limits and Continuity: 10-12%
- Differentiation: 35-40%
- Integration: 35-40%
- Sequences and Series: 12-18%

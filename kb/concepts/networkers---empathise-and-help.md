# Networkers   Empathise And Help

- Networkers

- Empathise and help others first
  - When meeting successful people, they do not think: "what can this person do for me?"
  - Think about ways others could truly benefit
  - Search for shared interests, asking questions of other person, and forge common ground
  - Have fun – they like excitement of working with others, as it enlarges possibilities

---

## Effective Networking Should Include

- Professional allies
- Weaker ties and acquaintances

---

## Effective Networking

- The best professional network is both narrow/deep (strong connections) and wide/shallow (bridge ties)
- Where there are alliances, strong/diverse trusted connections, access to fresh information, broad weak ties, and ease with which you get introduced to new connections

---

## How to Enhance Your Networking?

### Customise Your Approach
- When young/fresh, many weaker connections in disparate fields may help you
- With some experience, deepening alliances/connections in niche areas may be valuable

### Nurture Your Network
- Give and take – do something for someone, even if you are not needed
- Believe you can help others, regardless of your power, wealth, and/or experience

---

## How to Strengthen/Maintain Your Network?

### Help Others to Help Yourself
- Introduce people, and experiences, to one another
- Ask yourself: "What are the skill-sets, interests, and experiences, in a few domains, that I can connect, and help others, with?"

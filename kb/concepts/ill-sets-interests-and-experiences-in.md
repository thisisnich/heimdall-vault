## How to Network with Powerful People?

- Pay attention to relative/dynamic status
- In asking for a meeting – make it more convenient for higher-status person, whether it is time, and/or location
- Understand social power dynamics
  - E.g. not outshining/diminishing others (especially unintentionally)
- Don't make unsolicited offer to tell others how they could improve
  - It may lead to resentment, rather than appreciation

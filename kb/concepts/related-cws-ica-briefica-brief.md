| Criteria | Excellent | Very Good | Good | Satisfactory | Needs Improvement |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Content and Organisation** *(15 marks)* | Shows comprehensive knowledge of subject matter. Organised in a logical sequence, with points that are well-elaborated. Adequately interesting, relevant, and meaningful. | Shows some knowledge of subject matter. Organised in a logical sequence, with some elaboration.

## Networking Definition

Networking is the process of establishing and maintaining professional relationships that can provide support, information and opportunities.
(Saatchi, Pallotti, & Sullivan, 2023)

### Types of Networking
- **a) Internal Networking:** Building relationships within your organization
- **b) External Networking:** Connecting with professionals outside your organization
  (Haruta & Tsugawa, 2021)

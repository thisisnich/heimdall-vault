# An effective negotiator:
![Picture 3](Picture3.jpg)
Prepares and plans well
Knows his subject
Thinks reflectively, and clearly
Expresses his thoughts well
Listens, and clarifies
### Notes:
Has good judgment
Has integrity
Is persuasive
Is patient
Is decisive
<!-- Slide number: 12 -->
# An effective negotiator does not:
![Picture 4](Picture4.jpg)
Prepare sloppily
Push for a harsh deal
Intimidate
Lose his patience
### Notes:
Lose his temper
Talk excessively, to the detriment of listening
Argue and threaten, rather than trying to persuade and influence
Ignore conflict

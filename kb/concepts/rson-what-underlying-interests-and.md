# Rson What Underlying Interests And

- rson?
What underlying interests, and personal needs, does the other person have?
Which of those interests, and needs, coincide with yours?
What can you trade, to meet those common interests?
![Picture 4](Picture4.jpg)
### Notes:
*Always consider both organizational and personal needs in negotiations

# Be a learner in your negotiations
### Notes:
Learn to be a little more competitive, when you stand up for something you strongly believe in, even if it leads to a minor confrontation
Learn to yield, in order to accommodate more interests
Learn to ‘avoid/withdraw’, If you feel the matter is small, or that dialogue may be better another time
Learn to compromise, when time is little, and you need to accept an imperfect solution, for the time being
Learn to cooperate and/or collaborate, if you have the time and energy, to seek an answer that best meets all needs

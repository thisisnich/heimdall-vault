Networking at the Workplace
Relevance
Networking at the Workplace
- 1. Project Outcomes:
Improves and enhances project outcomes by allowing quick access to expert opinions
and resources.
- 2. Collaboration:
Promotes interdisciplinary collaboration, improving overall project delivery and
customer experience.
- 3. Professional Development:
Encourages continuous learning and staying updated with the latest development,
new methods, processes and technology.
(Jacobs, De Vos, Stuer, & Van der Heijden, 2019)
Networking Concepts
Enhancing Connections
Networking Concepts
- 1)Relationship Building:
a. Build trust and respect
-   Form genuine connections with working
team members, superiors and colleagues from
other departments.
b. Work closely as a team
-   Daily collaboration strengthens teamwork
and support
c. Improve project outcomes
-   Strong relationships lead to better
communication and improved project
outcomes.
(Rajamani & Iyer, 2023)
Networking Concepts
- 2) Communication Skills:
a. Listen actively:
• Understand others' concerns and perspectives
during handovers or team discussions.
b. Share clearly:
• Use concise, accurate language when reporting
project updates or changes in situation.

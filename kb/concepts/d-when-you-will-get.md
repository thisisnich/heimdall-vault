# D When You Will Get

- d when you will get back with the results.
Take fast action! Get the problem fixed. Resolve the misunderstanding. Champion the cause of your customer. Give more than what customer expects.
Finally, go back to the customer and explain how the problem has been resolved. Ensure he is fully satisfied, and thank him for allowing you to help.

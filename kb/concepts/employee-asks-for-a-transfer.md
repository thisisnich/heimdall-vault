# Employee Asks For A Transfer

- employee asks for a transfer to another department. Positional bargaining will focus on whether there is a vacancy, and on the employee's eligibility. What would you do if you were the manager?

### Activity 2: Great Questions
What are some examples of great questions you can ask as skilful negotiators? Remember great questions should be more open than closed, to identify needs, explore options, and develop agreement.

### Activity 3: Recent Negotiation
Think of a recent negotiation:
- What was the stated position of the other person?
- What underlying interests and personal needs does the other person have?
- Which of those interests and needs coincide with yours?
- What can you trade to meet those common interests?

# Tudents Are Reminded That Use

- tudents are reminded that use of artificial intelligence or any automated writing assistance tools is prohibited in the completion of this assignment. Copying another person’s work without proper referencing is plagiarism. Sharing of your work with others is also **not** permitted. Such acts may be subjected to disciplinary action.
Any assignment not received **by the stated deadline** will be deemed as a late submission. Based on NYP regulations: Marks will be deducted - 5% of the total base marks allocated for the assignment will be deducted for each calendar day the assignment is late. If the assigned work is not received **within three calendar days** following the due date, the assignment will be reviewed for feedback but will not be assigned a mark or grade.

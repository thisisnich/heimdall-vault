# Omes Others Into Your World

- omes others into your world, and appreciating opportunity to enter theirs. “Good morning”, “Nice to see you”, “Thanks for letting me serve you!”, etc. Heartfelt human spirit that uplifts!
<!-- Slide number: 18 -->
# PROVIDING GOOD SERVICE IS ALSO ABOUT LEARNING TO BE A GOOD CUSTOMER
Be appreciative and polite
Get your service provider’s name and use it
Be upbeat
Provide information just the way they want it
Confirm next actions
When appropriate, commiserate
Show your appreciation
![Picture 4](Picture4.jpg)
### Notes:
He or she is a human being just like you
Make a personal connection
For great vibes
To be fair
To be clear and show you truly understand
Be empathetic
“Thank you” is great for mental health.
<!-- Slide number: 19 -->
# What’s in it for yoU(if you provide good service)?
You will enjoy more compliments
Happy customers come back and tell others
Good for your organisation = good for your job security
Others will more likely step up for you (when you step up for them)
Workplace becomes more satisfying for all
![Picture 5](Picture5.jpg)
### Notes:
The world will uplift you, because you uplift the world.
<!-- Slide number: 20 -->
# Let’s Discuss
Where can you take more personal responsibility at work, at home, and in your life? How?
Which styles of service are you most comfortable providing?
How can you choose the best style to use, with different customers, and in different situations?

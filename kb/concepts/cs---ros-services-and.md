## 🎯 Key Concepts

### ROS (Robot Operating System)
- Middleware for robot software development
- Node-based architecture
- Topic-based communication (publisher/subscriber)
- Tools: RViz, Gazebo, rqt_graph

### SLAM
- **Simultaneous Localisation and Mapping**
- Localization: knowing where you are
- Mapping: creating a map of the environment
- Technologies: GPS, LiDAR, vision sensors

### Robot Components
- **Sensors:** Camera, wheel encoders, IMU, LiDAR, ultrasonic, infrared
- **Actuators:** Motors for movement control
- **Computing:** Microcontrollers, SBCs (Raspberry Pi), GPUs, Industrial PCs

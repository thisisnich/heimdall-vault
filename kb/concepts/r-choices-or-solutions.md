## Strategy 2: Use Power of Information

### The A.S.K. Framework
Before you ask for something, gather useful facts first. Remember: **A.S.K.**

- **A: Assess their needs**
  - Understand the Preferences, Priorities, and Options of the other party
  - What the other person wants or cares about

- **S: Study their options**
  - Know what choices they have or what they can offer

- **K: Know the norm**
  - Research what other workplaces usually offer for the same kind of job

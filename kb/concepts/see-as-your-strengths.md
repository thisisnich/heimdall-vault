# See As Your Strengths

- see as your strengths
- What topics would they need your advice on?
- How may you develop those strengths?

### Learn and Plan Adaptively
- Make plans based on your competitive advantages
- Leverage on your Assets, set yourself in direction of your Aspirations, and consider Market Realities

### Learn by Doing
- Ask yourself: "How may I grow my soft assets the fastest? How may I learn the most?"
- Do and try (through trial and error)

### Take Calculated Risks
- Be ready to learn from missteps, and recoverable failure
- Think two steps ahead
- What is more meaningful for you, in the longer run?

### Keep an Open Mind
- Establish an identity independent of your employer, location, and even industry
- Brand yourself personally, and/or asset-focused
  - E.g., "Entrepreneur, Marketing/Product Strategist, Investor, etc", not Job Title at Company A

---

## Draw Up an Action Plan

### Schedule a Coffee Meeting
- Meet with someone who used to work in your professional niche
  - How did he pivot to a new career?
  - Why did he do so, and was it a good move?
  - What were the signs that the time was right?

### Make a Plan to Develop More Skills
- That are useful to other jobs
  - E.g., writing/general management/tech/people skills, international experience, etc.
- Specific course, or 1 hour of self-learning each week?

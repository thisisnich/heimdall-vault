# Ing In Reflections Correctly

- ing in reflections. | Correctly applies some of the important elements in concepts in analysis and demonstrates some critical thinking in reflections. | Minimal attempts to apply concepts in analysis and/or reflections, with important elements misapplied and/or missing. Demonstrates no critical thinking in reflections. |
| **16 - 20 marks** | **14 - 15 marks** | **10 - 13 marks** | **0 - 9 marks** |
| **Structure and Organisation  (5 marks)** | Structure and organisation of the paper is very clear and coherent, and/or the mechanics of writing (e.g. grammar and spelling) is sound. | Structure and organisation of the paper is sufficiently clear and coherent, and/or the mechanics of writing (e.g. grammar and spelling) is acceptable. | Structure and organisation of the paper is somewhat clear but not very coherent, and/or the mechanics of writing (e.g. grammar and spelling) is comprehensible. | Structure and organisation of the paper is neither clear nor coherent, and/or the mechanics of writing (e.g. grammar and spelling) is incomprehensible. |

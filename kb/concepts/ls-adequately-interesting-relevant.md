# Ls Adequately Interesting Relevant

- ls. | Adequately interesting, relevant, and meaningful reflections and applications relating to module and negotiation, service and networking skills. | Uninteresting and irrelevant content which lack meaningful reflections and applications relating to module and negotiation, service and networking skills. |
| **Language and Coherence** *(15 marks)* | Very clear and comprehensible reflections and applications. | Mostly clear and comprehensible reflections and applications. | Sufficiently clear and comprehensible reflections and applications. | Adequately clear and comprehensible reflections and applications. | Incoherent and incomprehensible reflections and applications. |

|---|---|---|---|---|---|

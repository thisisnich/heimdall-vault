# L E A R N I N G   A C T I V I T Y !

• Think of a recent negotiation
• What was the stated position of the other person?
• What underlying interests, and personal needs,
does the other person have?
• Which of those interests, and needs, coincide with
yours?
• What can you trade, to meet those common interests?

Negotiation Strategy 1: Know Your BATNA
What is BATNA?
• Stands for ‘Best Alternative to a Negotiated Agreement’
What it Means?
• Means:  ‘What is your best backup plan if the answer is no?’

• Knowing your BATNA helps you stay calm, confident, and clear

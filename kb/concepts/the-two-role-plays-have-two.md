# The Two Role Plays Have Two

- the two role-plays, have **TWO** team spokespeople present on:

1. The reasons for your choice of the style(s) used in managing each of the scenarios. For example, why was the *Avoidance style* used? Was it because of the need to save time, or was it because of any particular workplace belief the characters hold, such as "The boss is always right"? You should refer to the unit on **Conflict Management** to prepare this part.

2. The key interpersonal skills used in managing the situation at each of the role-plays. For example, you can reference the verbal/non-verbal communication, listening skills and show of empathy, as shown in the role-plays. It is good to be specific and point out extracts of the conversations during the role-plays.

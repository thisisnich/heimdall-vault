# E Advantages While Working

- e advantages while working – what is your advantage in your industry? Intern, make connections, and explore methodically - test your market realities. Take actions - to discover where you want to go, and how to get there.
<!-- Slide number: 16 -->
# What other skills do you need for networking?
![Chart, diagram
Description automatically generated](Picture4.jpg)
Take calculated risks
Be ready to learn from missteps, and recoverable failure
Think two steps ahead. What is more meaningful for you, in the longer run?
### Notes:
E.g., if you would like to be promoted, it may mean a first step to build a relationship with a key partner, or taking up a course to pick up advanced skills, before asking for a promotion. Avoid immediate gratification and thinking too far ahead – you will change, the world will change, competition will change – plans may also need to change.

Establish an identity independent of your employer/location/industry
Brand yourself personally and/or asset-focused, e.g.: “Entrepreneur, Marketing/Product Strategist, Investor”, not Job Title at Company A
### Notes:
Own your professional identity - not moving only when something is not working.
Keep learning a new skill, building relationships across industry, interning, and having a side practice as Plan B (without big irreversible commitment).
